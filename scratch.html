Why I Love It

Three reasons why I love basketball:

  1. The Athleticism
    • Players combine speed, power, and coordination unlike any other sport.
    • Dunks, crossovers, and blocks never get old.
    • The physical IQ required is underrated.
  2. The Playmaking
    • Pick-and-roll coverage decisions happen in milliseconds.
    • Coaching adjustments between quarters can flip a series.
    • Analytics have added a whole new layer of appreciation.
  3. The Community is Unmatched
    • Supporters are passionate and loyal.
    • Teams foster a sense of belonging and pride.
    • Events bring people together in celebration.
